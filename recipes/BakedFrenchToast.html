Baked French Toast

Description

Indulge in the ultimate morning comfort with our Baked French Toast, a dish that transforms simple ingredients into a decadent, crowd-pleasing masterpiece. We start with thick slices of artisanal brioche, soaked in a rich, velvety custard infused with warm vanilla and a hint of ground cinnamon. By baking it to perfection rather than pan-frying, we achieve a delightful contrast in textures: a golden, slightly crisp exterior that gives way to a center so light and pillowy it practically melts in your mouth. 

Whether you are hosting a festive brunch or simply elevating your weekend routine, this dish offers a sophisticated twist on a classic favorite. Each bite is a harmonious blend of buttery sweetness and aromatic spice, best enjoyed with a generous drizzle of pure maple syrup and a handful of fresh, seasonal berries. It’s more than just a meal—it’s an invitation to slow down and savor a truly gourmet breakfast experience right at your own table.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, cut diagonally in 1 inch slices
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 1 ⅓ cups brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons light corn syrup

Steps

  1. Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish; arrange slices of bread on the bottom.
  2. Beat eggs, milk, cream, vanilla, and cinnamon together in a large bowl. Pour over bread slices, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
  3. The next morning, preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Meanwhile, combine butter, brown sugar, and corn syrup together in a small saucepan; heat until bubbling.
  4. Pour over bread and egg mixture.
  5. Bake in preheated oven, uncovered, for 40 minutes.
  6. Serve with berries and syrup.
